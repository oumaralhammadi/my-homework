Submit Your Event

Submit your event easily using the dedicated form on our platform.

  • Provide accurate details
  • Add event date & location
  • Include description
Review Process

All submitted events are carefully reviewed before publishing.

  • Review time: 24–72 hours
  • Content verification
  • Quality check
Publishing Criteria

Events must meet our quality standards before being published.

  • Clear and complete information
  • Academic relevance
  • No misleading content
Publication

Once approved, your event will be published and visible to all users.

  • Visible on platform
  • Users get notified
  • Can be shared easily
